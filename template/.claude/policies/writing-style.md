# Writing style

How prose and reports read in this project.

## Rules

- **Direct.** Lead with the point. Cut hedging, filler, and throat-clearing.
- **Concrete.** Prefer specific nouns, numbers, and examples over abstractions.
- **Scannable.** Use short paragraphs, lists, and tables. One idea per line
  where it helps the reader skim.
- **Honest.** State limits and uncertainty plainly. Do not oversell.
- **Evidence-bound.** Use labels and strong assertions ("proves", "confirms",
  "best") only when the evidence backs them; otherwise soften to match.
- **Present tense.** Describe what the code, data, or result does, not what it
  did or will do, unless time is the point.
- **US English.** Spelling, punctuation, and date conventions follow US usage.
